Notes
- Use .jpeg. If a file has the wrong name, rename the FILE, not the JSON.
- A stop with no photo shows a painted anchor placeholder; a team with no
  photo just shows its role as text. Nothing ever looks broken.
- Landscape looks best for stops; keep files under ~400 KB / ~1600 px long edge.
- After adding/changing photos, fully stop and re-run the app (`flutter run`) —
  assets are picked up at build start, not on hot reload.
